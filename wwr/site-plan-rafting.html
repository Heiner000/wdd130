Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to promote our rafting trips and services to potential customers. Here we will showcase our rafting locations, trip options, pricing, and possibly allow customer's to book reservations online. Our goal is to increase bookings and grow our customer base with an informative and user-friendly web presence. We will highlight our experience, expertise, and commitment to providing thrilling and safe whitewater adventures.

Audience

Who is our target audience? Our target audience are adventurous groups and individuals between the ages of 18 - 65 seeking an exhilarating and memorable outdoor experience.

What does our audience expect to find on our website? They expect to find information about our rafting locations, trip options, pricing, photos, waivers, reviews and maybe online booking.

How will our audience access our site? They will likely access our site both on desktop and mobile to view trip details, photos, FAQs, and make bookings, so it will need to be mobile-friendly with easy booking.
